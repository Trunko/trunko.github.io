Chess

During the Spring 2018 semester, I have been developing my own chess AI for my Introduction to Artificial Intelligence course using many different techniques. I have been implementing the AI using SIG-Game's AI framework that it uses for MegaMiner AI, however, instead of using simple tactics to implement a specific strategy, I have been trying to implement the Minimax search algorithm to play chess. Along with Minimax, I have also developed my own bitboard in order to speed up my AI's search time to have a better chance of winning the game.

My chess AI client was developed entirely in C#.
